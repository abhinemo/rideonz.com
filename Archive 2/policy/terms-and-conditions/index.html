Terms And Conditions

  • Users have to deposit a refundable security deposit for all the bikes (as mentioned during the booking) at the time of receiving the vehicle. The same shall be refunded to you at the time of returning the vehicle after deducting all the statutory charges/costs.
  • A valid driving license must be produced in original to the vendor at the time of receiving the vehicle.
  • Users should be wearing a Helmet and carry original driving license issued by the Government of India at all the times while driving the rented vehicle.
  • The pick up point and drop point will remain the same and the bike cannot be asked to be collected from a 3rd location unless pickup/drop facility is provided by the vendor. However, in case of emergency please call our helpline number (+91­ 9943130806).

  • Any physical damage / theft / loss to the bike will be the sole responsibility of the person renting the bike.
  • Bookings are subject to availability. Rideonz and its vendors reserves the right to cancel a booking if deemed necessary.
  • Please refer to ‘Order Summary’ page during the booking for vehicle or pickup location specific terms and conditions.

Cancellation Policy

  • In case of a cancellation, 100% fare is refundable if cancellation is done before 24 hours of pick-up time. A cancellation fee of 50% fare is applicable if the cancellations is done between 24-2 hours of pick-up time. Fare will not be refundable if cancelled within 2 hours of pick-up time or later.
  • If any booking is cancelled by Rideonz when a vehicle is not available or any other issues, full refund will be initiated.
  • All refunds will take around 7-10 business days to reach the customer's account depending on the bank.

Rescheduling & Extension Policy

  • All rescheduling requests are to be made at least 1 day before the pickup time and all extension requests are to be made at least 1 day before the drop off time. Such requests are to be made to customer care/ vendor over the phone. As per booking schedule, they may confirm or deny the request depending on the vehicle availability.
  • Original discounted rates won’t be applicable on trip extension.
